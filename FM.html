Description: FoodMate is an intelligent mess and canteen management system developed to streamline meal services in college campuses and hostels. It empowers students to pre-book meals, track occupancy in real-time, and experience smarter dining without queues.

Objective: To leverage AI and digital tracking to optimize food planning, minimize waste, and enhance the overall dining experience for students and staff.

Key Features:

  • Meal pre-booking based on student schedules
  • Real-time occupancy visualization to avoid peak-time crowds
  • AI-based predictions of food wastage for better planning
  • Admin dashboard for tracking usage, meals served, and feedback
  • Improves satisfaction by providing timely and quality meals

Benefits: FoodMate enhances operational efficiency, reduces surplus cooking, and helps institutions monitor food requirements with precision. It also saves student time and brings convenience to dining routines.

Technologies Used: Python (AI logic), JavaScript (UI), Firebase (optional real-time data)
